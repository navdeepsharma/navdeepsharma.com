Problems to solve

I'm a software consultant and product builder with a background in computer science and a career built across agile delivery, release management, and cross-functional product leadership.

My work sits at the intersection of technology and real human problems. I've led delivery at companies like Definity, Manulife Bank, and Innosphere — and I've taken everything I've learned in enterprise environments and applied it to building my own products.

I started Avasy Inc. as the vehicle for that work — an independent software company focused on building platforms and applications that solve problems people actually have. So far that's produced two products: Setu Sphere, a peer intelligence network for project management professionals, and Auxia, an AI-powered brain health app.

When I consult, I bring that same founder mindset — I'm not here to just fill a role. I'm here to help you build something that works.

How I work

A process built
for real outcomes.

Step one

Understand the problem

Before anything is built, I spend time understanding what you're actually trying to solve — not just what you think you need. The best solutions start with better questions.

Step two

Define the scope

Clear scope, clear expectations. I help define what's in and what's out — so the project has direction from day one and doesn't drift into scope creep.

Step three

Build and iterate

Agile delivery in practice. Short cycles, real feedback, continuous improvement. I build things that can be tested early and refined based on what actually works.

Step four

Ship and support

Getting to launch is only half the job. I help with go-live, handoff, and the post-launch period where most projects need the most support.
